Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast

Step

In large bowl, mix first 4 ingredients. Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes. Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes. Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan. Pat into a circle.

Topping: Mix first 5 ingredients and spread over crust. Put a few pepperoni slices on top of sauce. Sprinkle with 1/2 the mozzeralla; 1/2 the monterey jack, and 1/2 the parmesan. Put the rest of the pepperoni on. Repeat the cheese layer. Bake at 400* for 20 minutes or until light brown.
